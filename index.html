About me

I am Muntasir Ahmmed Muin, a dedicated Co-Founder, Managing Director, and Operations Executive with a proven track record of launching and scaling dynamic digital platforms. While I possess strong expertise in Web Development and UI/UX Design, my primary focus is on strategic leadership and building multi-sector businesses from the ground up.

Concurrently, as Co-Founder & COO of EZ-Shop, I oversee the full operational lifecycle of another specialized e-commerce venture, ensuring seamless customer experience and efficient business execution. My work here involves steering client success through best-in-class design, video production, web development, social media management, and strategic marketing consultation. My mission is to translate complex business challenges into elegant, functional, and user-centric digital solutions that deliver tangible growth and create lasting brand value.
